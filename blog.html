About The Page

The Idea behind this Webpage is to give fun meals for anyone to create together. It is ideally, more aimed towards couples of all ages to allow them to create these meals together as a fun couples activity for the evening. This Webpage offers a few recipes to make that range from short to long cook time, easy to medium difficulty and Starters, Mains and Desserts. We offer a decently sized variety of hearty meals for anyone to make fit for anyone to make





About The Recipes

The Recipes were chosen specifically for their simple yet elegant style, their diversity, not only in which part of the world they originate from but also their tastes, look and time they take to complete as something that takes too long or doesn't look good aren't really going to be all that fun to make. The Diversity helps people pick a food they may take more of an interest in the food. The Specific Recipes we chose for the Website are also the most commonly picked meals for couples on dates which gives it more intimacy to make these meals together.
