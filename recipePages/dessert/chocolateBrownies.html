Chocolate Brownies

Raiting:

Prep Time: 25 mins
Cook Time: 25 mins

Nutrition

Calories 150 kcal
Fat 9g
Fibre 1g
Protein 2g

Ingredients

  • 185g unsalted butter
  • 185g best dark chocolate
  • 85g plain flour
  • 40g cocoa powder
  • 50g white chocolate
  • 50g milk chocolate
  • 3 large eggs
  • 275g golden caster sugar

Methods

  1. Melt 185g unsalted butter and 185g dark chocolate in a bowl over simmering water or microwave for 2 minutes. Let it cool.
  2. Preheat your oven to 180°C/160°C fan/gas 4. Line a 20cm square baking tin with foil or parchment.
  3. Sift 85g plain flour and 40g cocoa powder into a bowl.
  4. Chop 50g white chocolate and 50g milk chocolate into chunks.
  5. Whisk 3 large eggs with 275g golden caster sugar until thick and creamy.
  6. Fold the cooled chocolate into the egg mixture gently. Then fold in the flour and cocoa mix. Stir in the chocolate chunks.
  7. Pour the batter into the tin and smooth the top. Bake for 25-30 minutes. It's done when the top has a shiny crust and the edges pull away from the tin.
  8. Let it cool completely in the tin, then cut into squares or triangles.
  9. Store in an airtight container for up to 2 weeks or freeze for up to a month.
