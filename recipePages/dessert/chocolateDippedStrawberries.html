Chocolate-dipped Strawberries

Raiting:

Prep Time: 20 mins
Cook Time: 4 mins

Nutrition

Calories 145 kcal
Fat 9g
Fibre 5g
Protein 2g

Ingredients

  • 100g dark chocolate, roughly chopped
  • 400g strawberries
  • 30g white and milk chocolate, roughly chopped (optional)

Methods

  1. Cut a strip of baking parchment and set aside. To melt the dark chocolate, heat 2cm of water in a small pan until simmering. Place the chocolate in a heatproof bowl on top of the pan, making sure it doesn't touch the water. Stir until melted, or melt in the microwave in 20-second intervals, stirring between each, until smooth. Transfer the melted chocolate to a small deep bowl.
  2. Hold the strawberries by the leafy tops and dip them into the melted chocolate. Gently tap off any excess chocolate and place the strawberries on the parchment to set. Any leftover chocolate can be poured onto another strip of parchment and left to set for later use.
  3. For added decoration, melt white and milk chocolate and drizzle over the dipped strawberries.
